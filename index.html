About the Firm

Rajagopal & Badri Narayanan (RNBCA) is a legacy Chartered Accountancy firm, founded in 1981. Headquartered in Bangalore with a branch in Chennai, the firm is a trusted partner to corporates, public institutions, banks, insurance companies, and non-profit organizations across India.

For over four decades, RNBCA has stood for professionalism, ethical conduct, and technical excellence in the domains of Audit, Taxation, Management Consulting, and Financial Advisory.
